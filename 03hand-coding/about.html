About

Founders

Meet the visionaries behind Bloom Apparel—two passionate leaders dedicated to crafting timeless luxury, exceptional quality, and a truly elevated fashion experience:

Elena Marceau
Co-Founder & Creative Director With a background in luxury fashion design and an eye for timeless elegance, Elena leads Bloom Apparel’s creative vision. Her designs blend modern silhouettes with refined detail, ensuring every collection feels elevated, confident, and enduring.
Julian Cross
Co-Founder & Chief Executive Officer Julian brings strategic leadership and a passion for craftsmanship to Bloom Apparel. With experience in luxury brand development and global retail, he oversees operations, growth, and the elevated customer experience that defines the brand.
Isabella Laurent
Co-Founder & Head of Design Innovation With a background in avant-garde fashion and luxury textiles, Isabella brings a bold, creative edge to Bloom Apparel. She drives experimental design, curates seasonal collections, and ensures every piece reflects the brand’s signature blend of elegance and modern sophistication.
